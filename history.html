Projects History. A short summary of the past few years

  • Present  Betalo

    I'm currently working as Lead Product Designer at Betalo in Stockholm, Sweden.

  • 2017  Glofox

    I worked with Glofox on a three month contract from July to October 2017. Glofox makes the world's best software for gym and studio management. I was responsible for overhauling the UI/UX design of the company's mobile apps for gym members as well as their admin apps for gym and studio owners.

  • 2017  Sportive Breaks

    I worked successfully with Sportive Breaks for 12 months in 2016 and 2017, assisting them in improving the design of their marketing and e-commerce sites. Sportive Breaks is a travel product, focused specifically on cycling sportives and cycling breaks in Europe. I was responsible for creating layouts and designs that would increase conversions, reduce bounce rates and make the process of booking a sportive or cycling break a pleasurable experience.

  • 2017  Review Solicitors

    ReviewSolicitors is a legal services comparison site operating in the UK and Ireland. I've helped them with the design of everything from widgets and emails to their analytical dashboards and review management products.

  • 2016  Viddyad

    I worked with ViddyAd on contract for 3 months in the summer of 2016, helping them to improve design on their marketing site and their video editing products.

  • 2016  W42St

    I worked remotely with W42St’s team in New York over several months to help them to bring their print magazine online, with a strong focus on user experience and editorial design.

  • 2016  UtilityAnswer

    UtilityAnswer is an energy startup in San Francisco that is upending the relationship between consumers and utilities companies. I worked with them over a period of 4 months, assisting with the design of a new product.

  • 2014 - 2015  Paradigit

    I worked for Paradigit in The Netherlands for 18 months between in 2014 and 2015, working as a UI/UX designer on their Irish and Belgian stores.

  • 2014  Radii

    I contracted with the Radii digital agency in Dublin for 4 months in early 2014, leading design and UX on a major learning app project.

  • 2012 - 2013  BuyersClub.ie

    BuyersClub was my first full time in-house role, working as a designer. I was responsible for all of the company's digital and print design. During my time with the company I received extensive training in user experience design from Jade O'Connor, the lead UX designer at Redeem and Get. I eventually worked with her in an effort to redesign the entire e-commerce platform from the ground up, working as UI lead.

  • 2010 - 2017  Various Freelance Work

    I started working as a freelance graphic and web designer in college at University College Dublin. Since then, I've worked with dozens of agencies, startups and small businesses not mentioned above, providing web and graphic design services.
